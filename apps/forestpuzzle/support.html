Frequently Asked Questions

I'm stuck on a word — what can I do?

Use a hint! Watch an optional ad to earn a hint that reveals a letter in the hidden word. The category clue shown at the top is also there to help.

How do I unlock Endless Forest Mode?

Complete all available levels to unlock Endless Forest Mode, which gives you infinite word puzzles to enjoy.

Can I play without internet?

Yes! Forest Puzzle is fully offline. You can play anywhere without an internet connection. Watching ads for hints requires a connection.

My progress disappeared — help!

Game progress is stored locally on your device. If you reinstalled the app or switched devices, progress may be lost. We recommend backing up your device regularly.
