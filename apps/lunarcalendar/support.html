Frequently Asked Questions

How do I switch between lunar and solar views?

Tap the toggle at the top of the calendar screen to switch between Lunar and Gregorian views.

How do I set reminders for festivals?

Tap on any festival or event and select "Add Reminder" to set a notification ahead of the date.

Which traditions are supported?

The app currently supports Vietnamese, Chinese, and general East Asian lunar calendar traditions.

The app shows the wrong lunar date. What should I do?

Ensure your device timezone is set correctly. If the issue persists, please contact our support team with your device model and OS version.
