Privacy Policy — HabitTracker

Last updated: March 2026

Son Software App ("we", "us", or "our") operates the HabitTracker mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.

To provide the habit-tracking experience, we may collect:

  • check_circle Usage Data: Anonymous app interactions and crash reports to improve the experience.

How We Use Data
